Oaten

Oat porridge for everyday breakfasts

Service

Visual Identity

Oaten is a modern porridge brand that begins in the field, where whole grain oats grow under open skies and journey from soil to spoon. Rooted in the grain and the ritual of breakfast, it brings honest, steady nourishment to everyday mornings.

Â© Oaten

Concept

A whole grain porridge brand rooted in the ritual of breakfast, connecting the journey from field to bowl through honest ingredients and grounded design.
Oaten celebrates the grain itself as both origin and symbol, expressing warmth, authenticity, and everyday nourishment.

Scope

Development of brand identity, packaging system for multiple flavours, flavour iconography, visual storytelling, and art direction across mockups and brand imagery.
Creation of a cohesive visual language balancing agricultural heritage with a contemporary, tactile aesthetic.
